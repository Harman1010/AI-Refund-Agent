AI REFUND POLICY

1. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase.

2. Customer must provide a valid order ID.

3. The order must exist in the CRM system.

4. Digital products are not eligible for refunds.

5. Clearance products are not eligible for refunds.

6. Products that have not been delivered are not eligible for refunds.

7. If all checks pass, the refund is approved.

8. Any policy violation results in a refund denial.
